Free and compulsory education to all children up to the age of fourteen is a constitutional commitment in India.
Yet, many children can’t afford to get educated, and don’t get very far with their lives. So, I made it my job, to help in providing free textbooks and resources to children, so that they too can learn, and gain the knowledge needed to move ahead with live, and become successful. These text books are a small step toward a brighter future. As Nelson Mandela once said,

“It is not beyond our power to create a world in which
all children have access to a good education”
